How it works

Cards Categories:



Corporate

This template design is for you to make invitation card suitable for large company or businesses. It is a formal way to invite a large companies or businesses to attend your company business event. It is in a long and persuasive or in a simple format.



Formal

This template is for you to make formal invitation card for formal event or ceremonies which follows a certain stipulated format. This type of letter is only suitable for official purpose to authorities, colleagues and seniors. This card is not necessary for personal contact, friends or family.



Informal

A template that is design for you to make informal invitation car. This type of card is used to invite close personal contact, family or friends to an event. This template is not suitable for official purpose or to be send to large companies.

How it works:

STEP 1:

Choose between 4 categories provided which is Corporate, Formal, Informal or Customize.

STEP 2:

Once you have selected, you will be directed to a template page. In this template page, there will be 2 types of template. Choose your preferred design or layout.

STEP 3:

Fill in the details/information to be displayed in your e-invitation card such as, venue, date, time, Etc.

STEP 4:

Once you have finished, you can preview your card and send it via emails.
